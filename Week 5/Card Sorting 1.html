Foldable.Me

Myself and a three other friends got togeher to perform this sorting. We all took out sticky notes and wrote words and phrases on different ones. Then after we wrote all the words that were relating to the site, we all used those sticky notes to create the sorting for the site. I sorted first and then my three other friends sorted. We all tried to sort the content of the site in ways very similar to the structure of the site and for it to still make sense.

The first sort was done by me. Looking at the foldabe website, I chose to go in the same format as the site and sorted using a very similar structure.

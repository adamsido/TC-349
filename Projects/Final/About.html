ABOUT US

Fillani Vintage is a clothing line that was founded by Tinu Adams at the age of 18 in the month of June, 2012. All clothes are selectively thrifted and handmade into very creative unique style clothing. For each piece of clothing there is only one design made so that no one one else will ever have the same type of design which makes everyone's outfit stand out and different from others. Goal : Establish Fillani Vintage and create a brand for it. Make Fillani Vintage bigger than it is, and involve investors who believe in the product. Expand Fillani Vintage's customers nationwide and accomplishing much more by 2013.
